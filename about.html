ABOUT US

LG Cleaning Services is a family run business, founded in October 2023.

Our company was first started to support the continuous running of my brother’s Air bnb’s as they were finding it difficult to find consistent and good cleaners. I started cleaning for my brothers in April 2023 to allow them time to find a cleaning company that is trustworthy, dependable and has the same values as they do. After a few months of cleaning my brothers Air bnb’s, their reviews got better and bookings increased by 40% in the first 3 months of me cleaning. Word got around in one of the apartment buildings about a new cleaner, as I was seen entering and exiting the building with cleaning equipment on a regular basis and we subsequently gained new clients. We then made a conscious decision to turn what was initially an intermittent cleaning service to support my brothers business’, to a family run business providing a great service to all.
